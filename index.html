SparkQuantum

Igniting youth interest and opportunities for a quantum-powered future.

Learn More

Created by students, for students

SparkQuantum is the first youth-led quantum nonprofit in the world to offer free, comprehensive courses for quantum physics and quantum computing targeted for students.

We are driven by the belief that age and financial situation should never be barriers to a quality education. By breaking down complex concepts, we help to nurture the next generation of innovators, enabling young minds to explore and envision the quantum-powered future.
